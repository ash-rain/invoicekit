[faq_tag]
FAQ

[faq_title]
Frequently asked questions

[faq1_q]
Is InvoiceKit truly EU VAT compliant?

[faq1_a]
Yes. InvoiceKit automatically calculates the correct VAT for every invoice based on your country, your client's country, and whether they have a valid EU VAT number. It handles standard rates, reverse charge, OSS, and non-EU exempt scenarios correctly.

[faq2_q]
Which EU countries are supported?

[faq2_a]
All 27 EU member states. VAT rates are hardcoded for BG, DE, FR, RO, PL, CZ, IT, ES, NL, PT, AT, BE, HR, HU, SE and all others. Rates are updated whenever EU legislation changes.

[faq3_q]
Does InvoiceKit support recurring invoices?

[faq3_a]
Yes. You can turn any invoice into a recurring one with weekly, monthly, quarterly, or yearly intervals. InvoiceKit automatically clones and issues the invoice on the due date — you don't have to touch it.

[faq4_q]
How does time tracking work?

[faq4_a]
Create projects with clients and hourly rates. Use the built-in start/stop timer while you work. All tracked time is stored and can be converted directly into invoice line items with one click.

[faq5_q]
Is there a free trial?

[faq5_a]
Yes. Every new account starts with a 14-day Pro trial — no credit card required. You get full Pro access from day one. After the trial you stay on the Free plan unless you choose to upgrade.

[faq6_q]
How do online payments work?

[faq6_a]
Connect your Stripe account in Settings → Payments. InvoiceKit generates a secure payment link for each invoice. Your client clicks Pay Now and pays by card. Funds land directly in your bank account — InvoiceKit never holds your money. A 2% platform fee applies to card payments. Bank transfers via IBAN are always free.

[faq7_q]
How does AI document import work?

[faq7_a]
InvoiceKit can read invoice and expense documents (PDF, JPEG, PNG) and automatically extract the vendor, amount, date, and line items using Google Gemini AI. Free plan users get :free_limit AI imports per day; Starter plan users get :starter_limit. Pro users have unlimited imports. On any plan, you can add your own free Gemini API key in Settings → AI to remove all limits.
